About the app

When working with people with dementia it is important to know how difficult situations and changes in behaviour can arise and how to work in a preventative way. This app will give you practical advice and tips on what you can do.

The app is part of an educational package *Zerovision – For a dementia care without restraint.

The app is part of an educational package *Zerovision – For a dementia care without restraint.

There is a handbook and a web based course for staff. In addition there is a pamphlet and an app specifically for next of kin and other carers (*the handbook “Zerovision – For a dementia care without restraint” and the apps are available in English, the rest of the package is only available in Swedish).

Publisher: Stiftelsen Svenskt Demenscentrum, 2015 The Swedish Dementia Centre, 2015
