Apathetic and indifferent

"Elsa sits quietly without caring about what is happening. She seems to have lost all interest and lacks motivation to take part in activities."

A doctor should check medication and determine possible depression. It is also important to eliminate pain or any other bodily cause. In order to break the apathy you need to encourage taking part in different everyday activities. A person with dementia has difficulty taking initiatives.

Tips:

- Use the life history to find useful information about the person.

- Make things easier and encourage participation in everyday activities.

- Try to help and encourage the person to make her own decisions.

- Praise and confirm. Using humour can be liberating.

- Ask one question at a time. Wait for an answer before asking the next question.

- Create contact by a gentle hand or foot massage using pleasant smelling skin cream. Try tactile massage.

- Try showing photos or things from the past which can arouse interest and participation.
