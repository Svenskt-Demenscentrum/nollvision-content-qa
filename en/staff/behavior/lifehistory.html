Life history

Conversing with a person who has dementia is not always easy. By talking to relatives you can get help with information about what is or has been important in the person’s life.

With the person’s life history as a base and the knowledge you have about the person it will be easier for you to work person-centred and prevent difficult situations from arising.

How do you prevent a situation from arising such as, for example, a person starts to pull at a door handle and wants to go home? From the example with Elsa, what would you have done?

Have you used the person’s life history or information from relatives at any time to avoid using force or restraint?
