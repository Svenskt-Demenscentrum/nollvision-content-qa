The Necessity law

In accordance with the Necessity law you may hinder a person who is about to walk out onto a busy street if there is no other way of preventing it. This also applies if someone is about to hurt him/herself or someone else. The Necessity law may not be used as a matter of routine e.g. when a person, every day, wants to go out.

The Necessity law can only be used in acute situations and under exceptional circumstances.
